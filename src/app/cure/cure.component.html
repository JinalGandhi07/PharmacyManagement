COVID-19 Precautions and Treatment

Precaution

1. Wash your hands or sanitize them using soap and sanitizers.

2. Maintain 1 meter distance from a person sneezing, and cover your mouth while coughing.

3. Avoid touching your face refrain from activities that weakens the lungs.

4. Avoid unnecessary travel, practice physical distancing and stay home.

Treatment At Home

If your symptoms are mild enough, then you can recover at home:

1. Rest and stay home.

2. Drink fluids and monitor your oxygen level timely.

3. Get help from your doctor about necessary medications and exercises.

Treatment At Hospital

Scientists all around the world are working to find the optimum treatment for COVID 19

Supportive care includes oxygen cylinders or severely ill patients and people who require advanced respiratory support.

Doctors may give you an antiviral medicine called remdesivir which is the first drug approved by the FDA for treatment of hospitalized COVID patients over the age of 12.
